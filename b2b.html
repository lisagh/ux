Created a "Newco", Squarer, which provides a home-based physical therapy IoT connection to the targeting users: the elderly patients and their caregivers.

4. Pain Points

After understanding the market and know who are the users, we summarized the pain points based on what we got.

  • How to connect the patient and the caregivers?
  • How to track the patient's status?
  • What's the way to manage the post-operation physical therapy at home?

Ideate

1. Brainstorm

Usually, after the patient received a knee replacement, the orthopedic surgeon and physical therapist will suggest him or her exercise for 20 to 30 mins, 2 to 3 times a day and walk around 30 mins as well. In order to have a safe recovery, it's necessary to check with the therapist or surgeon before performing any types of exercise.
After collecting all information that we need, as a team, we executed a brainstorming workshop to get the ideas that help to solve our problem. We explored different possibilities such as Xbox, motion tracker, healthcare scanner, smart glasses, smart slipper, and smart necklace. At the end of the ideation phase, we decided to go with the motion tracker and mobile app.

2. Conceptual Design

System Model
We tried to design an IoT wearable device for the elderly to track their daily and exercise motion which is easy to take and use. In this concept, there are three main functions:
1. the wearable device can detect several body conditions and upload the data to the server.
2. the system can deliver the physical therapy plan from the caregiver/therapist to the patient.
3. the caregiver can check the progress or get emergency alert of the patient in his or her devices.
4. considering the elderly memory recession, it would be useful to have a voice control widget as a reminder to tell the elderly when to exercise or do other things.
5. the IBM AI service can be served as the analyzer to generate the report and discover potential trend or change for the elderly patient.

Emergency care
We noticed that, if the elderly live alone and the emergency happened during the post-operation period, it's hard to get help from the caregivers or families in time. For example, when the patient doing physical therapy but accidentally fall down, how do the IoT device can help?
